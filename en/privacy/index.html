Privacy policy

Data controller

Guillaume Bassand — contact@enso-psychologie.ch

Data collected

This website does not actively collect any personal data. No forms, tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools are used.

The only data processed are standard access logs automatically collected by the hosting provider GitHub Pages (IP address, browser, pages visited, date and time). These are managed by GitHub Inc. in accordance with their own privacy policy.

Contact by email

If you write to contact@enso-psychologie.ch, your data (name, email address, message content) are used solely to respond to your enquiry. They are not shared with third parties and are not subject to any automated processing.

Retention period

Email exchanges are retained for as long as necessary for the professional relationship, then deleted.

Your rights

In accordance with the nFADP (new Federal Act on Data Protection, in force since 1 September 2023), you have the right to access, rectify and delete your personal data. To exercise these rights: contact@enso-psychologie.ch

Changes

This policy may be updated. The date of last revision is shown at the bottom of the page.
