I am a member of the Swiss Federation of Psychologists (FSP) and the Vaud Association of
Psychologists (AVP), two reference bodies that uphold strict ethical and professional
standards in psychological practice.

I am also a member of ACAMH — the Association for Child and Adolescent Mental Health — an
international scientific network based in the United Kingdom, dedicated to research and
clinical practice with children and adolescents.
