## Data collected

This website does not actively collect any personal data. No forms, tracking cookies
or third-party analytics tools are used.

The only data processed are standard access logs automatically collected by the hosting
provider GitHub Pages (IP address, browser, pages visited, date and time). These are
managed by GitHub Inc. in accordance with their own
[privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

## Contact by email

If you write to contact@enso-psychologie.ch, your data (name, email address, message
content) are used solely to respond to your enquiry. They are not shared with third
parties and are not subject to any automated processing.

## Retention period

Email exchanges are retained for as long as necessary for the professional relationship,
then deleted.

To exercise these rights: contact@enso-psychologie.ch
