## Who is this for?
For anyone going through a challenging or unsettling period — a life transition, a loss,
a change, a moment of doubt. But also for those who are doing well and simply want to
step back, understand themselves better, or work on personal growth in a structured way.

## What we work on together
Each session is a confidential space to explore what you are going through, at your own
pace. Depending on your situation, we may work on:
- Understanding your emotions and reactions
- Managing stress, anxiety or sleep difficulties
- Life transitions — professional, family or personal
- Building your inner resources and self-awareness

## My approach
I draw on tools from humanistic, cognitive behavioural and positive psychology traditions.
The goal is not to provide a diagnosis, but to offer concrete support towards greater
clarity and wellbeing.

## Practical information
- **Duration:** 60 minutes

- **Online** — invoiced in advance, session confirmed upon receipt of payment
