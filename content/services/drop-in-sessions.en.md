## Who is this for?

For people who need a space to talk at a particular moment, without necessarily
committing to regular sessions. This is especially suited to those waiting to begin
ongoing therapy — a wait that can feel long, and that you do not have to go through alone.

## What we work on together

One or a few focused sessions, based on what you need right now:

- Getting through a difficult moment or a short-term crisis
- Taking stock before an important decision
- Preparing for an event or a transition
- Finding support while waiting to begin ongoing therapy

## My approach

These sessions are flexible by nature. They can remain drop-in or evolve into more
regular support if that suits you better. No long-term commitment is required from
the outset.

## Practical information

- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Fee:** CHF 150
- **In person** in Lausanne
- **Online** — invoiced in advance, session confirmed upon receipt of payment
- Sessions are not covered by basic Swiss health insurance (LAMal). Partial reimbursement
may be available through supplementary insurance — please check with your provider.
